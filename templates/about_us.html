About Us

February 24, 2025 • by Admin

United Kolkata Sports Club (UKSC) is a dynamic football club and academy based in Kolkata, West Bengal, founded in 2024 with a mission to nurture and develop young footballing talents. UKSC serves as a comprehensive academy and institution, providing aspiring footballers with a structured pathway from grassroots to the professional stage. Guided by the vision of Director Debdut Roychowdhury, UKSC is committed to raising the standards of football development and competition. As he puts it, “UKSC is an institution with a solemn promise to elevate sports to the highest standards.” In its debut year, UKSC made an unprecedented impact, hosting champion men’s and women’s teams that dominated the Calcutta Football League First Division Championship—one of the most remarkable performances by a newly formed club. With a relentless drive for excellence, UKSC aspires to compete in the Indian Super League (ISL) in the near future, establishing itself as a powerhouse in Indian football.
