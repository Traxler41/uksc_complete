Sameer Pradhan Makes History with Sikkim State Team Selection for Santosh Trophy

February 24, 2025 • by Admin

Sameer Pradhan has etched his name in the history of United Kolkata Sports Club (UKSC) by becoming the first player from the club to earn a call-up for the Santosh Trophy. The young defender was selected to represent the Sikkim Football Association (SFA) in the 78th edition of the Santosh Trophy, marking a significant milestone in his career.

Pradhan featured in all three group-stage matches for Sikkim, making appearances as a substitute. He played against Manipur, Tripura, and Mizoram, gaining valuable experience on a big stage. His consistent performances and dedication have been commendable, reflecting UKSC’s growing influence in Indian football.

The Santosh Trophy remains one of the most prestigious tournaments in Indian football, and Pradhan’s selection is a testament to his talent and hard work. His journey from UKSC to the state team highlights the academy’s commitment to nurturing future stars.

UKSC congratulated Pradhan on his achievement, expressing pride in his progress. With this feat, he has set a benchmark for aspiring footballers at the club, inspiring many to dream big and push for national recognition.
