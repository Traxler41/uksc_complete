A black day for United Kolkata Sports Club

March 03, 2025 • by Admin

The celebrations surrounding United Kolkata Sports Club’s (UKSC) remarkable inaugural season in the Calcutta Football League (CFL) came to an abrupt halt following the sudden passing of the club’s goalkeeping coach, Prashanta Dey. The 41-year-old, a key figure in shaping the success of head coach Deepak Mondal’s squad, tragically passed away on November 16, 2024.

The incident occurred around 2 PM at Salt Lake’s Central Park, where Dey was conducting a training session for the Bidhannagar Municipal Corporation Academy. Shortly after the session began, he collapsed on the field. As an immediate effort to revive him, he was rushed to a nearby hospital at approximately 3:30 PM, where he was pronounced dead. The cause of death was confirmed as a massive cardiac arrest.

Earlier that morning, Dey had conducted a separate training session before heading to Central Park. His sudden demise has been a significant loss for UKSC, as he had been closely associated with the team since the academy of the CFL season. The football community mourns his loss, with tributes pouring in to honor his dedication to the sport and his players.
